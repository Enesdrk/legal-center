Welcome to Chibi Survivors. We are committed to protecting player privacy and only collecting data needed for game operations.

1. Information We Collect

  • Device Information: Model, OS version, and language settings.
  • Gameplay Data: Progress, unlocked levels, and in-game economy values.
  • Crash Logs: Anonymous diagnostics to fix bugs.
  • Advertising Signals: iOS ATT consent state, Android Advertising ID, rewarded ad measurement.

2. Third-Party Services

  • Unity Ads: Rewarded advertisement delivery.
  • RevenueCat + App Store / Google Play Billing: In-app purchase handling and restore flows.

3. How We Use Data

  • To provide optional rewarded ads requested by players.
  • To keep progression and balancing metrics functional.

4. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If this occurs, contact us and we will delete applicable data.

5. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of eligible data. For cloud-related requests, contact support.

6. Changes

Material policy updates are reflected by changing the date on this page and, where required, in-app notice.
